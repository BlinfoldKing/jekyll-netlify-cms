About Us

PT Dimensi Gagas Prima is a full-service interior design specializing in Corporate, Banks and multi-international company office planning, design and project management. The most important product of our work is our clients’ satisfaction. We are totally committed to it. Together with each client, we work to provide an appropriate solution to their requirements. We attribute our success in fulfilling our clients’ goal to our commitment to placing one of our principals in the role of “project principals” to manage and control all phases of each individual project.Our hallmark of service, thoroughness, integrity accountability and quality design result in lasting clients relationship.
